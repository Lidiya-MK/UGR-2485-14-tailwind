Seneca

Seneca a famous philospher once said "While I may wish to be free from the pangs of suffering and adversity, should the hour demand its presence, I am resolved to confront it with an unwavering spirit. I shall aspire not for its absence but for the strength to endure it with fortitude, to navigate through pain without surrendering my tranquility or causing harm to others." It is precisley because of this and many other sayings I find great value in his teachings.
